  • Loterya

    Loterya reimagines Loteria with a uniquely Filipino twist—built using Godot and scripted in GDScript.

    It all started with a folk dance during our midterms in Philippine Arts—every turn and stride brought us closer to the richness of Filipino culture. Inspired by this experience, we created Loterya as a celebration of our heritage.

    This multiplayer game reimagines the traditional game of Loteria, integrating mechanics similar to Bingo while infusing vibrant Filipino-inspired imagery. Iconic symbols such as the barong, sampaguita, and halo-halo come together to form a unique visual narrative.

    The immersive soundscape features traditional Filipino melodies and festive rhythms, enhancing the connection to cultural roots. Loterya, developed with Godot and powered by GDScript, is more than just a game—it’s an invitation to celebrate Filipino heritage and create unforgettable memories.
